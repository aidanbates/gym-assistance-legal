# MyFitLab Support

Welcome to MyFitLab support. We're here to help you get the most out of your fitness tracking experience.

## Getting Started

### First Time Setup
1. Download MyFitLab from the App Store
2. Create your account with email and password
3. Set your preferred units (kg/lbs, km/miles)
4. Allow location permissions for run tracking
5. You're ready to track your first workout

### Quick Start Guide

**Starting a Workout**
- Tap the Workout tab
- Choose "Quick Workout" or select a template
- Add exercises from our library
- Log your sets, reps, and weights as you go
- Rest timer automatically starts between sets

**Tracking a Run**
- Tap the Running tab
- Press "Start Run"
- Your pace, distance, and route are tracked automatically
- Swipe up to pause or end your run
- View detailed stats and map after completion

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Workouts

**Q: How do I create a custom workout template?**
A: Go to Workout tab > Templates > Create New. Add your exercises, set default sets and rest times, then save with a name.

**Q: Can I edit a workout while I'm doing it?**
A: Yes. You can add or remove exercises, change weights, and modify sets during any active workout.

**Q: What does the flame icon mean?**
A: The flame indicates this is a warmup set. Warmup sets are tracked separately from working sets.

**Q: How do rest timers work?**
A: Rest timers automatically start after completing a set. You can skip them anytime or adjust default rest periods in your templates.

### Running

**Q: Why isn't GPS working?**
A: Make sure location permissions are set to "Always" or "While Using App" in your phone's Settings > Privacy > Location Services > MyFitLab.

**Q: Can I run without internet?**
A: Yes. GPS tracking works offline. Your run will sync when you reconnect.

**Q: How accurate is the distance tracking?**
A: GPS accuracy depends on signal strength. For best results, wait for GPS lock before starting and avoid areas with tall buildings.

### Progress Tracking

**Q: How do I view my personal records?**
A: Go to Profile > Analytics to see your PRs for each exercise including highest weight, most reps, and total volume.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes. Profile > Settings > Export Data will create a file with all your workout history.

**Q: What are progression charts?**
A: Tap any exercise in Analytics to see graphs of your strength gains over time.

### Account & Settings

**Q: How do I change units from pounds to kilograms?**
A: Profile > Settings > Units. Changes apply to all future and past workouts.

**Q: Is my data backed up?**
A: Yes. All data syncs to the cloud automatically when connected to internet.

**Q: Can I use MyFitLab on multiple devices?**
A: Yes. Sign in with the same account on any device to access your data.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**App crashes during workout**
- Force close the app and reopen
- Your workout is auto-saved and can be resumed
- If issues persist, try deleting and reinstalling

**Rest timer notifications not working**
- Check notification permissions in Settings
- Ensure Do Not Disturb is off
- Volume must be on for timer alerts

**Exercises not saving**
- Check your internet connection
- Pull down to refresh the screen
- Try logging out and back in

**GPS route not showing**
- Location services must be enabled
- Check GPS signal strength
- Restart your phone if GPS isn't responding

### Error Messages

**"Network Error"**
Your workout is saved locally and will sync when connection returns.

**"GPS Signal Lost"**
Move to an open area for better satellite reception. The app continues tracking with last known speed.

**"Session Expired"**
Your login has timed out for security. Sign in again to continue.
