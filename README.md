## Common Questions

### Getting Started

**How do I start my first workout?**
1. Open the app and tap the Workout tab
2. Choose "Quick Workout" to start immediately
3. Add exercises by tapping "Add Exercise"
4. Enter your weights and reps for each set
5. Tap the checkmark when you complete a set

**How do I track a run?**
1. Go to the Running tab
2. Tap "Start Run"
3. The app tracks your route, pace, and distance
4. Swipe up to pause or finish

**What are workout templates?**
Templates are pre-made workout routines you can reuse. Create your own or use the built-in ones like Push Day, Pull Day, or Leg Day.

### During Your Workout

**What's the rest timer?**
After you complete a set, a rest timer starts automatically. It helps you rest the right amount between sets. You can skip it anytime.

**Can I change weights during a workout?**
Yes! Just tap the weight field and enter a new number. The app saves all changes automatically.

**What does the flame icon mean?**
The flame marks a warmup set. These don't count toward your working sets but are still tracked.

**How do I add more sets?**
Tap "Add Set" below any exercise to add another set. You can also add warmup sets.

### Tracking Progress

**Where can I see my personal records?**
Go to Profile > Analytics to view all your PRs including heaviest weight lifted and most reps completed.

**How do I view past workouts?**
Profile > Workout History shows all your completed workouts. Tap any workout to see the full details.

**Can I see my running history?**
Yes, go to Running tab > History to see all past runs with maps, pace, and distance.

### Settings & Account

**How do I change from pounds to kilograms?**
Profile > Settings > Units. You can switch between lb/kg and miles/km.

**Is my data saved?**
Yes, everything syncs to the cloud automatically when you have internet. You can also work out offline.

**Can I export my data?**
Profile > Settings > Export Data creates a file with all your workout history.

## Troubleshooting

### The app crashed during my workout
Don't worry! Your workout is auto-saved. When you reopen the app, you'll see an option to resume.

### GPS isn't working for runs
1. Check that location permissions are enabled in your phone settings
2. Make sure you're outdoors with clear sky view
3. Wait a few seconds for GPS to lock before starting

### My exercises aren't saving
- Pull down on the screen to refresh
- Check you have internet connection
- Try logging out and back in

### Rest timer notifications aren't working
Check that notifications are enabled for MyFitLab in your phone's settings.

### The app feels slow
- Close and reopen the app
- Make sure you have the latest version
- Free up some phone storage if it's full

## Tips for Success

### Workout Tips
- Log your weights immediately after each set for accuracy
- Use the notes feature to remember machine settings
- Create templates for your regular routines to save time
- Review your history to ensure progressive overload

### Running Tips
- Wait for GPS lock before starting for accurate distance
- Use the pause feature for traffic lights or breaks
- Check your splits to monitor pacing
- Save routes you enjoy to repeat them

### Progress Tips
- Take progress photos in Profile to track visual changes
- Check Analytics weekly to see strength trends
- Celebrate PRs when you hit them
- Use the progression charts to spot plateaus
