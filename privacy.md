# Privacy Policy for Gym Assistance

**Last Updated:** January 20, 2026

## Introduction
Gym Assistance ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

## Data We Collect
- **Account Information:** Email address, display name
- **Workout Data:** Exercise logs, sets, reps, weights, workout sessions
- **Body Metrics:** Optional weight entries
- **Health Data:** Steps, heart rate (via Apple Health/Google Fit - processed locally only)

## How We Use Your Data
- Provide core workout tracking functionality
- Calculate statistics and progress metrics
- Sync data across your devices
- Improve app performance and features

## Data Storage & Security
- All data stored in encrypted Supabase PostgreSQL database
- Row-Level Security (RLS) ensures your data is isolated
- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Health data from Apple Health/Google Fit is processed locally and never uploaded to servers

## Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)
- **Access:** View all your data at any time
- **Export:** Download complete data export (coming soon)

## Data Retention
- Active accounts: Data retained indefinitely
- Deleted accounts: All data permanently removed immediately

## Third-Party Services
- **Supabase:** Database and authentication hosting
- **Sentry:** Error tracking (anonymized)
- **Apple Health/Google Fit:** Optional integrations (data stays on device)

## Children's Privacy
Gym Assistance is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

## Changes to Privacy Policy
We will notify users of material changes via email or in-app notification.
